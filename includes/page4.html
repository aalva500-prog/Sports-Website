Liverpool Football Club is a professional football club
that competes in the English Premier League.


Founded in 1892, Liverpool has won six European Cups,
more than any other English club.




Mohamed Salah Hamed Mahrous Ghaly is an
Egyptian professional footballer who plays as a forward for Premier League club Liverpool.


In 2019, he finished as the Premier League's joint-top goalscorer,
and helped Liverpool win the 2019 UEFA Champions League.
