Juventus Football Club is an Italian professional association
football club based in Turin, Piedmont.


Founded in 1897 by a group of Torinese students,
the club's fan base is the largest at national level and one of the largest worldwide.




Cristiano Ronaldo dos Santos Aveiro is a Portuguese professional
footballer who plays as a forward for Serie A club Juventus.


Ronaldo has won 29 trophies in his career, including six league titles,
five UEFA Champions Leagues,one UEFA European Championship, and one UEFA Nations League.
